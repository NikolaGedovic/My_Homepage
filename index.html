About Me

Hello! I'm Nikola. After years of working in factories, I discovered my passion for web development through my love for gaming and curiosity about the digital world. In 2023, I began learning front-end development, and that's when my journey truly began. It's incredibly fulfilling to craft content in a text editor and witness it come to life in the browser!
